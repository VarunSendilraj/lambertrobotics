Build

Building is an important aspect of robotics, and is one of the foundational ideas that all of us must use, albeit in different ways. We share our ideas so that we can promote one another to be successful.

Innovate

Here at Lambert it's about being unique. We believe that introducing new methods, ideas, or designs in something established will allow us to achieve success.

Compete

Competing is where all of the hard work pays off. We put all our hard work on the field and let the robot speak for itself.

Our Values

Teamwork

Working as a team is the best way to solve problems.

Work Hard

Working hard is what leads everyone to success.

Help Others

Helping others is a way to learn and get involved in the community.

Have Fun

Having fun is an important aspect of robotics.
